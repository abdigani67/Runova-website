The problem we kept seeing.

Across UK businesses — independent car dealers, aesthetic clinics, and plenty besides — the same thing keeps happening. Someone messages at 9pm asking about a car, a treatment, a price. That message waits until morning. And by morning, that person has usually gone with whoever replied first.

That's not a customer service failure. It's a structural gap. No business owner can be available 24 hours a day. But the enquiries keep coming regardless — on WhatsApp, Instagram, and web forms.

Runova was built to close that gap. Not with a clunky chatbot that sends scripted responses and frustrates people, but with a real AI assistant that has a genuine conversation, quotes real prices, answers real questions, and sounds like your own team.

We started with two industries we could go deep on: Runova Auto for independent car dealers, and Runova Aesthetics for UK aesthetic clinics. Each is a done-for-you setup with no technical effort on your part. You tell us about your business and we take it from there.

If you're losing leads because enquiries go unanswered after hours, Runova is built for you.
