The problem we kept seeing.

Aesthetics clinics are run by talented, busy people, practitioners who are booked back-to-back, who care deeply about their clients, and who are genuinely great at what they do. But when someone messages their Instagram at 9pm asking about lip fillers, that message waits until morning. And by morning, that person has usually booked somewhere else.

That's not a customer service failure. It's a structural gap. No clinic owner can be available 24 hours a day. But the Instagram DMs keep coming regardless.

Runova was built to close that gap. Not with a clunky chatbot that sends scripted responses and frustrates customers, but with a real AI receptionist that has a genuine conversation, quotes real prices, answers real questions, and sounds like the clinic's own team.

We're based in Bristol, we're ICO registered, and every clinic we work with gets a done-for-you setup with no technical effort on their part. You tell us your treatments and we take it from there.

If you're losing bookings because DMs go unanswered overnight, Runova is built for you.
