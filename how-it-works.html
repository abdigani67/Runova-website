The process

From enquiry to booked
in seconds, not hours.

Here's exactly what happens when a patient messages your clinic on Instagram, and what you see on your end.

Book a free demo
Step 01: Setup

You fill in one form. We do the rest.

Getting started takes five minutes on your end. You tell us your treatments, prices, and booking link. We handle the technical setup, connecting to your Instagram, training the AI on your clinic's tone, and going live.

Most clinics are up and running within 48 hours of submitting their details.

  • Share your treatment menu and prices
  • Tell us your booking link
  • Describe your clinic's tone (or we'll match your existing Instagram voice)
  • We connect, configure, and go live
  • You get access to your dashboard
Step 02: Answering DMs

Every DM answered in seconds. Every time.

When a patient messages your clinic, whether it's 2pm or 2am, Runova replies instantly. It knows your treatments, your real prices, and how to sound like your team.

It handles price questions, treatment information, booking requests, and common objections, all in your clinic's own voice.

  • Responds in under 5 seconds
  • Quotes real prices with £ signs, never says "contact us"
  • Handles multiple conversations simultaneously
  • Knows when to step back and flag your team
  • Sends your booking link at exactly the right moment
Step 03: Lead scoring

Every lead scored before you see it.

After each conversation, Runova scores the lead: Hot (ready to book), Warm (interested but needs nudging), or Cold (just browsing). Your dashboard shows you the hot ones at the top, so your team knows exactly where to focus their time.

No more trawling through DMs trying to figure out who's serious.

  • Hot, asked about price, wants to book soon
  • Warm, interested, asking questions, needs a nudge
  • Cold, browsing, no clear intent yet
  • Scores update in real time as conversations evolve
  • Hot leads always appear at the top of your dashboard
Step 04: Your dashboard

A full pipeline view, updating in real time.

Log in and see every enquiry, their lead stage, estimated treatment value, and the full conversation. Your conversion funnel is visible at a glance, from new enquiry to treatment sold.

Your team only needs to step in for the conversations that truly need a human touch.

  • Pipeline value tracked across all enquiries
  • Full conversation history per lead
  • One-tap Sold / Lost outcome tracking
  • 14-day enquiry chart
  • Accessible from any device

Tone matching

FAQ

Common questions.

Will it sound like a robot to my customers?
No, that's the most common concern, and the one we take most seriously. The AI is trained specifically on your clinic's tone. Most patients can't tell it isn't a real person. And the alternative is no reply at all, which definitely loses them.
What if it gives a customer wrong information?
The AI only knows what you tell it, your real treatments, your real prices. It doesn't invent anything. If someone asks about a treatment that isn't on your list, it says the team will confirm. You can read every conversation in your dashboard.
I already reply to my own DMs. Why do I need this?
You probably do a brilliant job when you're available. This is for the 10pm message, the day you're back-to-back with clients, the Saturday afternoon rush. It's a safety net for the enquiries that currently wait hours or go unanswered.
How long does setup take?
Five minutes of your time filling in a form. We handle the technical setup, and most clinics are live within 48 hours.
Is my customers' data safe?
Yes. Runova is ICO registered. Each clinic's data is completely isolated, nobody else can see your enquiries. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We both sign a Data Processing Agreement before you go live.
Can I see what the AI is saying to my customers?
Yes, every conversation is visible in your dashboard in real time. You can read exactly what was said, when, and how the lead was scored. Nothing is hidden.
